Recommended fraud analytics use-cases which can be carried out on ECHS beneficiary claims data 
1. Hospital Revenue Inflation & Behavioural Shift Analysis
- Claim vs Approved variance
- OPD/IPD ratio shift
- Emergency admission spikes
- High pharmacy billing %
Predictive
: Flag hospitals likely to inflate bills next FY
2. Procedure-Level Upcoding Detection
- Diagnosis vs procedure mismatch
- Sudden rise in specific packages
Predictive
: Identify hospitals shifting toward high-value packages
3. Doctor-Level Referral & Admission Pattern Analysis
- IPD conversion rates
- Referral clustering, Family and repeat referrals in same year patterns
- Unusual admission patterns
Predictive
: Identify referral syndicates
4. Beneficiary-Level Utilisation Outliers
- Claims frequency
- High-cost recurring treatments
- Multi-hospital usage
Predictive
: Flag misuse or collusion of 
beneficiary
 and Hospitals
5. Repeat Admission Analysis
- Readmissions within 7/15/30 days
- Same diagnosis repetition
Predictive
: Detect staged admissions
6. Pharmacy Billing Abuse
- % pharmacy component
- High-cost drug deviations
Predictive
: Identify over-prescribing
7. Emergency Admission Misuse
- % emergency admissions
- Time-of-day clustering
Predictive
: Detect misuse of emergency route
8. Package vs Item Billing Anomaly
- Package vs item mismatch
- Double billing
Predictive
: Detect billing manipulation
9. Geo-Spatial Fraud Clustering
- Fraud hotspots
- Cross-region referal usage
Predictive
: Identify emerging clusters
10. Time-Series Surge Detection
- Monthly spikes
- End-of-quarter surges
Predictive
: Detect artificial billing spikes
11. Duplicate Claims Detection
- Repeated claims
- ID duplication
Predictive
: Detect identity misuse
12. Hospital Speciality Misuse
- Non-core procedures
Predictive: Detect scope creep
13. High-Value Claim Risk Scoring
- ML-based scoring
Predictive: Real-time fraud detection
14. Pre-Authorization Deviation
- Approved vs actual variance
Predictive: Detect cost escalation
15. Length of Stay Manipulation
- LOS vs norms
Predictive: Detect unnecessary admissions
16. Diagnostic Test Overutilisation
- Excessive testing
Predictive: Detect inflation
17. Network Collusion Detection
- Beneficiary-doctor-hospital links
Predictive: Detect fraud rings
18. Claim Processing Delay Analysis
- Delay vs fraud correlation
Predictive: Prioritize audits
19. Policy Abuse Patterns
- Rule exploitation
Predictive: Policy redesign inputs
20. Budget Impact & Leakage Estimation
- Fraud leakage %
Predictive: Forecast expenditure
